About the Firm

DRP & Co. LLP

A firm of Chartered Accountants registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), established in 1995 and providing audit, tax, advisory, and wealth management services from Pune, Maharashtra.

The Firm

Established in 1995

DRP & Co. LLP is a firm of Chartered Accountants registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India under Firm Registration Number W100047. The firm has been in continuous practice since 1995, providing professional services across audit and assurance, tax advisory, business valuation, financial advisory, techno-economic studies, corporate governance, and wealth management.

The firm operates from its head office in Pune, Maharashtra, serving clients across multiple industry sectors including banking and NBFCs, manufacturing, real estate, renewable energy, pharmaceuticals, IT and services, agri-business, and infrastructure.

The team comprises Chartered Accountants, Cost Accountants, Registered Valuers, Certified Wealth Managers, DISA-qualified professionals, and specialists in forensic audit and IFRS. The firm serves a diverse client base ranging from listed companies and banks to SMEs and startups.

Our Approach

How We Work

01

Standards-Based Practice

All engagements are conducted in accordance with Standards on Auditing, ICAI Valuation Standards, International Valuation Standards (IVS), Ind AS, and other applicable regulatory frameworks.

02

Multi-Disciplinary Expertise

The firm brings together Chartered Accountants, Registered Valuers, Cost Accountants, Forensic Auditors, and IFRS specialists under one practice to deliver integrated advisory.

03

Sector Understanding

Professionals are aligned to specific industry sectors — banking, manufacturing, real estate, energy, pharma, IT, and agri-business — enabling focused domain knowledge.

04

Technology-Enabled

The firm uses structured internal systems including TimeWise EMS for engagement tracking and a Document Management Portal for secure client document handling.

05

Client Relationships

Engagement partners are directly involved in service delivery, ensuring consistent quality, timely communication, and continuity across multi-year engagements.

06

Continuous Development

The team regularly participates in ICAI CPE programmes, technical updates, and professional development to stay current with evolving regulatory requirements.
