About the Firm

Providing audit, tax, and advisory services since 1995

DRP & Co. LLP is a firm of Chartered Accountants registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (FRN: W100047). The firm provides audit and assurance, tax advisory, wealth management, business valuation, corporate governance, and techno-economic study services.

The team of 35+ qualified professionals includes Chartered Accountants, registered valuers, and specialists across finance, taxation, and compliance domains, serving clients from Pune, Maharashtra.
